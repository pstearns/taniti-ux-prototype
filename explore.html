Explore Taniti

Discover the natural beauty and vibrant culture of our island.

Natural Beauty

From the depths of the ocean to the peaks of the mountains.

Pristine Beaches

Taniti boasts both white sandy beaches and dramatic rocky coastlines encircling Yellow Leaf Bay.

Lush Rainforests

Explore the verdant tropical rainforests through guided hikes and experience diverse flora and fauna.

The Active Volcano

A must-visit highlight—witness the majestic, small active volcano in the mountainous interior.

Merriton Landing

Located on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay, this rapidly developing area is the heart of Taniti's entertainment.

Activities & Adventures

From peaceful sightseeing to adrenaline-pumping adventures.

Water Sports

Dive into crystal clear waters with snorkeling, or join one of our professional chartered fishing tours and boat tours around Yellow Leaf Bay.

Island Adventures

Experience the thrill of zip-lining through the lush tropical rainforest or take a breath-taking helicopter ride for a bird's eye view of the island.

Sightseeing & Culture

Visit our local history museum, explore native architecture in Taniti City, or take a guided bus tour to see the island's most popular sights.

Coming Soon: Professional Golf

We are excited to announce that a premier nine-hole golf course is currently under construction and is expected to be fully operational by next year!
