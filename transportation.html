Ground Transportation

Choose the mode of transport that best fits your pace.

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Taxis & Rentals

Taxis are available in Taniti City. Rental cars can be hired from a local agency near the airport.

Bikes & Foot

Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors. Taniti City and Merriton Landing are very walkable and easy to explore on foot.

Note: Helmets are required by law.
